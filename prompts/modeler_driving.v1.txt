You are given a route through stops with time windows. There are {n} stops
indexed 0 to {n_minus_1}, with stop 0 the start.

Coordinates (x, y):
{coord_lines}

Service time at each non-start stop is {service_time}. The start has no service
time. Travel time between stop i and stop j is round(distance(i, j) / {speed}).

Time windows [open, close], inclusive, per stop:
{window_lines}

A route starts at stop 0 at time 0, visits every stop once, and returns to stop
0. The vehicle may wait at a stop until its window opens and cannot arrive after
its window closes. Service time is charged when departing a non-start stop. The
goal is the completion time back at the start from time 0.

Call the solve_tsp_tw tool with the coordinates, time windows, service time, and
speed read from this problem. Do not solve it yourself.
